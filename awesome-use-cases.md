# Awesome Dotnetflow Use Cases

Real-world workflow engine scenarios.

## Business Process Automation

- **Invoice approval chains**: Route invoices through multi-level approvals with escalation timers.
- **Employee offboarding**: Orchestrate account deactivation, equipment return, and exit interview scheduling.
- **Contract lifecycle**: Track contracts from draft through review, approval, signing, and renewal.

## DevOps & Platform Engineering

- **Deployment pipelines**: Coordinate build, test, staging deploy, approval gate, and production rollout.
- **Incident response**: Trigger runbooks automatically when alerts fire, with human-in-the-loop escalation.
- **Infrastructure provisioning**: Orchestrate VM creation, DNS setup, SSL cert issuance, and monitoring config.

## Data Engineering

- **ETL orchestration**: Coordinate extract, transform, validate, and load steps with retry and dead-letter handling.
- **Data quality checks**: Run validation rules across datasets and route failures to data stewards.
- **Report generation**: Schedule data aggregation, chart rendering, and email distribution.

## Customer Success

- **Onboarding workflows**: Guide customers through setup steps with progress tracking and SLA alerts.
- **Renewal management**: Trigger renewal reminders, usage reviews, and upsell proposals automatically.
